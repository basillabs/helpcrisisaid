In 2010, Crisis Aid bought property once used as a brothel in East Africa. We turned it into Mercy Chapel, a recovery home for sex trafficking survivors.

  • Counseling: From this safe refuge, girls begin recovering from trauma.
  • Vocational training: Girls learn skills like leatherworking, hairdressing, and computer skills. Upon graduation, we help them find new jobs.
  • Church: Over 90% of the girls graduating from Mercy Chapel receive Jesus as their Lord and Savior.

Join us in changing these lives. We've rescued and graduated over 300 girls, and with your support, we'll help many more.
